# Roadmap (V2)

While V1 of SplitNest handles the core workflows for shared living (expenses, chores, AI assistant, settlements), we are already planning V2 to make the platform even more seamless and powerful.

### Planned Features

1. **Native iOS & Android Applications**
   - React Native / Expo application for native push notifications and widget support.

2. **Plaid Integration (Bank Syncing)**
   - Automatically import transactions from your bank account and easily assign them to your apartment expenses.

3. **In-App Payments**
   - Direct ACH transfers between roommates inside the app using Stripe Connect, eliminating the need to use Venmo or PayPal manually.

4. **Advanced Chore Rotation**
   - Automated complex rotation algorithms (e.g. assigning harder chores based on point values to ensure absolute fairness over time).

5. **Landlord Portal**
   - A dedicated portal for landlords to collect rent directly through SplitNest, and for roommates to submit maintenance requests.

6. **Internationalization (i18n)**
   - Complete multi-language support and dynamic currency conversion for global users.
